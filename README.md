## 🤖 AI Web Scraper with MCP — Built for Claude

![AI & MCP](assets/screenshot-1-1.png)

Ultimate Web Scraper **speaks MCP** (Model Context Protocol) — the open standard that lets AI assistants connect to your tools. Connect your cloud workspace to Claude and other AI agents, then scrape and work with your data conversationally:

- 🔌 **Connect Claude** (web and desktop) with a guided setup, plus access tokens for **Claude Code**, **Cursor**, and any MCP-compatible client
- 💬 **Chat with your data**: *"Which of the 2,000 companies I scraped don't have an email?"* or *"Clean up this table and merge the address columns."*
- 🪄 **AI-started extractions**: ask your assistant to extract product data, emails, social links, phone numbers, or Google Maps places
- ⚙️ **Operate automations**: re-run a price-monitoring or lead-gen scrape and get told when it's done — all in natural language
- 🛡️ **Workspace-scoped and secure**: read-only or full-access tokens, revocable anytime; agents only ever see your cloud data

AI connections are **rolling out now** for cloud workspaces. → [Connect an AI agent (MCP)](https://ultimatewebscraper.com/docs/ai/connect) · [AI Agents & MCP overview](https://ultimatewebscraper.com/docs/ai)
